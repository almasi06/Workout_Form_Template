Workout Details







Workout Ideas:

    Yoga

  • Duration: 30–90 minutes per session, depending on intensity and type.
  • Calories Burned: 150–500 kcal per hour (depends on style; e.g., Hatha burns less than Power Yoga).
  • Best Time: Morning (for energy and focus) or evening (for relaxation)
  • Improves: Flexibility, balance, and some strength.

    Hiking

  • Duration: 1–5 hours, depending on trail difficulty
  • Calories Burned: 350–700 kcal per hour (varies with incline and backpack weight).
  • Best Time: Morning or late afternoon (to avoid extreme heat).
  • Improves: Endurance, strength (especially legs and core), and balance.

    Running

  • Duration: 20–60 minutes per session.
  • Calories Burned: 600–1,000 kcal per hour (depends on speed and intensity).
  • Best Time: Morning (boosts metabolism) or evening (improves performance)
  • Improves: Endurance, strength (legs and core), and some balance.

    Swimming

  • Duration: 30–60 minutes per session.
  • Calories Burned: 400–700 kcal per hour
  • Best Time: Morning (energizing) or afternoon (muscle recovery).
  • Improves: Endurance, strength (full-body workout), and flexibility.

    Cycling

  • Duration: 30–90 minutes per session.
  • Calories Burned: 400–1,000 kcal per hour (depends on speed and resistance).
  • Best Time: Morning (fresh air) or late afternoon (better endurance).
  • Improves: Endurance, strength (legs), and balance.

    Walking

  • Duration: 30–60 minutes per session.
  • Calories Burned: 200–400 kcal per hour.
  • Best Time: Morning (refreshing), after meals (aids digestion), or evening (relaxing)
  • Improves: Endurance, balance, and light strength (legs).

    Gyming

  • Duration: 45–90 minutes per session.
  • Calories Burned: 300–700 kcal per hour (depends on exercise type).
  • Best Time: Morning (boosts metabolism) or evening (higher strength levels).
  • Improves: Strength, endurance, and some balance
